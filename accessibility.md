# Accessibility Statement

**The Sift** is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

### Standards
We strive to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. Our minimalist design is intended to be screen-reader friendly and easy to navigate via keyboard.

### Feedback
If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact us at: [access@thesift.com](mailto:access@thesift.com)
