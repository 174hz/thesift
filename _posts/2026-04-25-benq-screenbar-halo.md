The BenQ ScreenBar Halo transcends the functional realm of monitor lighting, achieving the status of a meticulously crafted desktop sculpture. Its matte black aluminum alloy construction exudes a quiet confidence, perfectly complementing any modern workspace. The wireless controller, a weighty, tactile delight, adds an element of sophisticated control that belies its diminutive size.

Performance is equally impressive. The Halo delivers a precisely calibrated beam, eliminating glare on the monitor surface while casting a soft, ambient glow that reduces eye strain during extended use. The color temperature adjustments are seamless and nuanced, ranging from a cool, focused white to a warm, inviting amber. The rear ambient light, a subtle touch, adds a layer of depth to the viewing experience, subtly illuminating the wall behind the screen.

BenQ has clearly poured attention into every detail. The magnetic mounting system is secure and unobtrusive. The build quality is exceptional; there are no flimsy components or cheap plastics in sight. This isn't merely a light; it’s an investment in visual comfort and aesthetic refinement. The ScreenBar Halo is a premium product that delivers on its promise, seamlessly blending form and function into a truly exceptional desktop accessory. A must-have for discerning users.
