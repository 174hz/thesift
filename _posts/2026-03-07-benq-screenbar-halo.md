The BenQ ScreenBar Halo isn't just task lighting; it's a statement. Its elegant, matte black aluminum construction and subtly curved form exude a premium feel, seamlessly integrating into any modern workspace. The wireless controller, weighty and tactile, adds another layer of sophistication, its intuitive dial offering precise control over brightness and color temperature. It's a masterclass in understated industrial design.

Beyond aesthetics, the Halo truly shines. The triple lighting system - forward, ambient backlighting, and focused downlighting - creates a balanced, glare-free illumination that significantly reduces eye strain during long hours at the screen. The automated dimming feature, utilizing the ambient light sensor, intelligently adjusts brightness to maintain optimal viewing comfort. Furthermore, the color temperature range, from a cool daylight to a warm amber hue, allows for perfect customization to match individual preferences and ambient conditions.

The attention to detail is palpable. The precisely engineered clip ensures secure and balanced placement on the monitor, avoiding any wobble or obstruction. The beam angle is expertly calibrated to minimize reflections and hot spots, delivering a consistently even and comfortable light distribution. The ScreenBar Halo is a testament to meticulous craftsmanship, a superior lighting solution that elevates both the visual experience and the overall aesthetic of the workspace. For those who demand the best, the Halo is an investment worth making.
