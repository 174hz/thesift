The BenQ ScreenBar Halo isn't merely a desk lamp; it's a masterclass in understated elegance and functional design. Its unibody aluminum construction whispers of meticulous craftsmanship, a stark contrast to the plasticky alternatives flooding the market. The matte black finish exudes sophistication, seamlessly integrating into any modern workspace without clamoring for attention.

Performance is where the Halo truly shines. The curved light bar casts a wide, flicker-free glow, eliminating eye strain during long hours spent in front of the screen. The intuitive wireless controller offers precise adjustments to brightness and color temperature, allowing for a personalized lighting experience tailored to the task at hand. The ambient backlighting adds a touch of visual flair, reducing contrast between the screen and the surrounding environment for enhanced comfort.

Beyond its core functionality, the Halo showcases attention to detail that elevates it above the competition. The weighted clip ensures a secure fit on even the thinnest monitors, while the thoughtfully designed cable management system keeps your desk clutter-free. This isn’t just illumination; it’s an investment in both productivity and well-being, a testament to BenQ’s commitment to quality and innovation. It's a minimalist masterpiece that delivers on its promise of superior screen lighting.
