The Elgato Stream Deck MK.2 isn't just a peripheral; it's a statement. From the moment you unbox it, the subtle heft of the device speaks volumes about its build quality. Button customization is effortless, the intuitive software allowing for a seamless mapping of actions to its fifteen keys.
