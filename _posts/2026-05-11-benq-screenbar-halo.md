The BenQ ScreenBar Halo isn’t just a desk lamp; it’s a testament to meticulous design. From the sleek, minimalist form factor to the seamless integration with your monitor, its presence elevates the entire workspace. The Halo's wireless controller, sculpted from a cool-touch metal, is a tactile joy, offering intuitive control over brightness, color temperature, and the backlighting feature, which paints a subtle, ambient glow on the wall behind.

Performance is equally impressive. The asymmetrical optical design throws a focused, even light across the desk, eliminating glare and reducing eye strain during prolonged screen time. Color accuracy is superb, rendering text and visuals with remarkable clarity. The built-in ambient light sensor intelligently adjusts the ScreenBar's output to maintain optimal brightness in any environment.

The craftsmanship shines through in every detail. The sturdy aluminum construction feels substantial and durable, while the precision engineering ensures a secure and stable fit on virtually any monitor. The magnetic attachment allows for easy adjustments and repositioning, further highlighting the thoughtful design.

The ScreenBar Halo is a premium product with a premium price tag, but it delivers a tangible improvement to your work environment. It's an investment in your well-being, a statement of discerning taste, and a benchmark for what a modern desk lamp should be. A true masterpiece of industrial design.
