The Asus ROG Strix Scope II 96 Wireless is a masterclass in space-efficient keyboard design. By utilizing a "96% layout," Asus has managed to include the essential number pad while keeping the footprint barely larger than a standard tenkeyless board. This layout is a boon for productivity users who require the numpad for data entry but want to maintain a minimalist desk aesthetic and ergonomic mouse positioning.

The typing experience is defined by the NX Snow switches, which come pre-lubricated for a "thocky," premium feel right out of the box. Integrated dampening foam reduces ping and echo, making the keyboard quiet enough for open-office environments without sacrificing tactile satisfaction. One of the standout features is the multi-function wheel and button, which can be mapped to volume control, media playback, or even screen brightness.

Wireless performance is rock-solid via the ROG Omni receiver, which can connect multiple devices to a single dongle.
