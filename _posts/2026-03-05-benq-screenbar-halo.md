The BenQ ScreenBar Halo transcends the mundane, becoming a meticulously crafted object of desire. From the moment it's unboxed, the industrial design whispers of understated elegance. The matte black, subtly textured aluminum alloy feels substantial, exuding a premium quality often missing in desktop accessories. Performance is equally impressive. The Halo casts a precisely calibrated light, banishing screen glare without harshness. The craftsmanship is evident in every detail.
