The BenQ ScreenBar Halo is not merely a desk lamp; it's a meticulously crafted statement piece that elevates the entire workspace. From the unboxing experience to everyday use, the attention to detail is palpable. Its industrial design is a masterclass in minimalist elegance: the brushed aluminum finish, seamlessly integrated touch controls, and the perfectly weighted, compact controller exude quality.

Performance is equally impressive. The auto-dimming feature, calibrated to ambient light, delivers consistent and comfortable illumination, reducing eye strain during extended screen time. The adjustable color temperature, ranging from warm amber to cool white, allows for customized lighting to suit any task or mood. The back light, a subtle yet impactful addition, adds a sophisticated ambiance without reflecting on the screen.

BenQ’s craftsmanship shines through in every aspect. The build quality is exceptional; the ScreenBar Halo feels substantial and durable. The intuitive clip mechanism securely attaches to any monitor without scratching or obscuring the display. Even the included cables are thoughtfully chosen and neatly bundled. This is a premium product that delivers on its promise, offering not just functional lighting but a refined aesthetic and a tangible improvement to the overall computing experience. It’s an investment in both productivity and visual harmony.
